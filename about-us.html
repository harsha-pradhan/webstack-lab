Sweetness is a must in life

You’ll see the stove’s open fire and feel the glow from our broilers working throughout the day. They’re the customs we set up years prior that we still enthusiastically pursue today.

Enlivened by incredible crisp fixings, our chain of Cake bakery shop which is now all across India, started making craftsman breads and naturally prepared desserts at a small kisok in Tawal nagar in Mumbai. Also, upon a little achievement, neighbors started to approach us for sandwiches made with that awesome bread, trailed by hand crafted soups and servings of mixed greens, and even made-to-arrange scramblers.
